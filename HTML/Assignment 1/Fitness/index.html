Symptoms of PCOD

It is generally noted that PCOD starts showing symptoms around the time of the first menstrual cycle, at the time of puberty, but it certainly develops with time, leading to weight gain over the years. Some of the common signs of PCOD include: Rise in androgen levels: It pertains to high levels of male hormones that can lead to excessive facial and body hair, as well as male-pattern baldness. Irregular periods: One of the most experienced symptoms. Many females experience irregular or delayed periods due to problems with egg maturation.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

It is a backbend that stretches the chest, shoulders, and spine while strengthening the back muscles. Here’s how it benefits us-

How to do it:

Firstly, lie on your stomach, place your palms under your shoulders, and slowly lift your chest while keeping your lower body grounded. Hold for 15–30 seconds.

Benefits:

It balances thyroid function. Strengthens the hips, core, and spine.

Tips for Practicing Yoga for PCOD
